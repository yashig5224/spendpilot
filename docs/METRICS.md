# METRICS

## North Star Metric

The main thing we care about is ** Audits Per Week**. This is because SpendPilot is a tool that helps people find products and generates leads. When someone finishes an audit it means they:

* get what SpendPilot is about

* trust SpendPilot enough to give us the information we need

* get to the main result of the product.

For a business like ours it is more important to have people finish audits than to just have a lot of people visiting the site.

---

## Input Metrics

### 1. Landing Page → Audit Start Conversion

This measures how well our landing page gets people to start the audit.

We want least 25% of people to start the audit after they visit the landing page.

---

### 2. Audit Completion Rate

This measures if people can finish the audit without getting stuck.

We want least 70% of people to finish the audit.

---

### 3. Audit → Lead Capture Conversion

This measures if people think the audit is valuable enough to give us their contact information.

We want between 15% and 20% of people to give us their contact information.

---

## What I Would Instrument First

First we need to track what people are doing on the site. We should track:

* how many people visit the landing page

* how many people click on the call to action

* how many people start the audit

* how many people finish the audit

* how many people give us their contact information,

* how many people share the results with others.

This will help us see where people are getting stuck.

---

## Pivot Threshold

If we are not doing well we might need to change our plan. This could happen if:

* than 10% of people finish the audit

* people keep quitting before they get to the results

* No one shares or looks at the results again

* than 5% of people give us their contact information even after we try to fix things.

This would mean that people do not think the problem is important or that our suggestions are not helpful.

For a business, like ours it is more important to have people engaged and finishing audits than to just have a lot of people visiting the site.
