# USER_INTERVIEWS

## Interview 1

**Name:** R.G.

**Role:** Technical Founder

**Company Stage:** Pre-seed SaaS startup

### Key Quotes

* "We are paying for ChatGPT, Claude and Cursor at the time because nobody wants to give up these tools.”

* "I am really not sure which AI subscriptions are actually worth the money anymore.”

* "Our OpenAI API bill randomly doubled month and we did not notice until finance flagged it.”

### Surprising Insight

R.G. Said they keep using overlapping AI tools on purpose because their developers really like different workflows even if it costs more. The AI tools are like R.G.s AI tools. They want to keep all of them.

### What It Changed

I added support for detecting overlapping subscriptions of thinking companies only use one AI platform at a time. R.G.s interview also changed the way I explain recommendations in the results dashboard. Now it is more about R.G.s AI tools. How they can be used.

## Interview 2

**Name:** A.K.

**Role:** Freelance Developer

**Company Stage:** Solo / Indie Hacker

### Key Quotes

* "I subscribe to tools when I see demos on Twitter it is like a buy.”

* "I probably spend more on A.K.s AI tools than my hosting bill, which's A.K.s main expense.”

* "I would use something like this to check if my A.K.s AI tool subscriptionsre okay.”

### Surprising Insight

A.K. Did not care that much about saving money but more about knowing if A.K.s current setup with A.K.s AI tools is reasonable or not. A.K. Wants to know about A.K.s AI tools.

### What It Changed

I added text to explain recommendations and focused less on suggesting downgrades. The audit results are now like advice, rather than just trying to save money on A.K.s AI tools. A.K.s AI tools are important to A.K..

## Interview 3

**Name:** S.P.

**Role:** Engineering Lead

**Company Stage:** Series A Startup

### Key Quotes

* "Nobody is in charge of AI spend yet so S.P.s AI tools are not managed.”

* "Different teams are using different AI tools, like S.P.s AI tools.”

* "Finance knows about AWS costs but not S.P.s AI tooling costs, which is a problem for S.P.s AI tools.”

### Surprising Insight

S.P. Explained that AI software spending is often all over the place with reimbursements, team subscriptions and API usage making it hard to see what is going on with S.P.s AI tools. S.P.s AI tools are not easy to track.

### What It Changed

This made me structure SpendPilot around showing everything in one place and making reports that summarize things of just giving recommendations for individual AI tools like S.P.s AI tools. Now it is more, about S.P.s AI tools. How they can be managed.
