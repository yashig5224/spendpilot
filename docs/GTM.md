# GTM

## Target User

The main person we are trying to help is an engineering manager or technical founder at a startup with 5 to 40 employees. These teams use a lot of intelligence tools like Cursor, Claude and ChatGPT Team but they do not have a good way to see if they are spending their money wisely.

A typical user would be a CTO at a YC startup an engineering lead at an AI SaaS company or a technical founder who manages the software budget themselves. These users really care about saving money. They also want to be as productive as possible.

---

## Trigger Moments

When users are about to look for SpendPilot they are usually doing things like checking their SaaS invoices noticing they have multiple AI subscriptions that do the same thing talking about reducing their burn rate searching for the best AI coding tools for startups comparing Cursor vs Copilot vs Claude or checking their OpenAI API costs because they got a surprisingly high bill.

They are often thinking to themselves: Are we paying much for AI tooling?

---

---

## First 100 Users (30 Days $0 Budget)

1. We will personally send messages to 5-10 startup founders every day on X and offer them a free AI spend audit.

2. We will post content that breaks down how a startup is wasting $800 per month on AI tools.

3. We will share examples of optimization in communities like Indie Hackers, Reddit and founder Slack groups.

4. We will launch on Product Hunt with a free AI Spend Score.

5. We will build a public calculator that users can share with others.

6. We will reach out to college founder communities and hackathon groups that are already using AI tools a lot.

We are focusing on talking to founders instead of trying to market to a large audience.

---

## Unfair Distribution Channel

The strongest advantage we can have is to use the startup founder and engineering communities that are already talking about AI tooling every day. Since SpendPilot helps with AI software overspend it can fit naturally into conversations about productivity reducing burn rate and engineering efficiency.

A company like Credex could also help us distribute the tool through their existing relationships with startups and procurement workflows.

---

## Week-1 Traction Goals

If people like our product we hope to see:

* 150 or more visitors to our landing page

* 40 or more completed audits

* 10-15 leads

* 3-5 consultation conversations

* least 1 founder sharing their audit results publicly

The main sign of success will not be how much traffic we get but whether founders are willing to share their results and talk about savings opportunities, with their teams.
