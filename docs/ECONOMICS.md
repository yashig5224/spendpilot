# ECONOMICS

## Converted Lead Value

If Credex uses SpendPilot, a converted lead could be worth between $500 and $3000. This depends on the relationship that comes after.

We are making a guesses here.

* We think 10 percent of people who use the audit will book a consultation.

* Then we think 20 percent of those consultations will turn into a financial services relationship.

* We also think that the average annual revenue per converted startup customer is $2,500.

If we do the math we get this:

100 audits

will lead to 10 consultations

which will lead to 2 converted customers

and that is $5,000 in annual revenue.

This means that each completed audit is worth about $50.

## CAC by Channel

### Founder DMs on X

* The cost is $0.

* It takes a lot of time. It is highly targeted.

* We think the estimated CAC is $20 to $40 in founder time.

### Indie Hackers / Reddit

* The cost is $0.

* We make posts and hope people see them.

* We think the estimated CAC is $10 to $25.

### Product Hunt

* The cost is $0.

* We have to get ready for the launch and talk to the community.

* We think the estimated CAC is $50 because the people who convert are not as good.

### College Founder Communities

* The cost is $0.

* A lot of people in these communities use AI tools.

* We think the estimated CAC is than $10.

## Funnel Economics

Let us say we have a funnel that looks like this:

1,000 visitors

will lead to 250 completed audits (25 percent)

will lead to 40 consultation requests (16 percent)

will lead to 8 paying customers (20 percent)

If each customer gives us about $2,500 in revenue

then 8 customers will give us $20,000 in annual revenue.

If we do not have to pay much to get these customers

then the economics become attractive quickly.
